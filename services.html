Pool Services in Rio Verde, Arizona

Comprehensive pool care designed for Rio Verde's unique desert climate

Weekly Pool Cleaning & Maintenance

Our most popular service keeps your Rio Verde pool in perfect condition all year long. Each weekly visit includes skimming the surface, brushing walls and tile, vacuuming the floor, emptying baskets, testing and balancing water chemistry, and inspecting all equipment.

Arizona's intense sun and dust storms mean your pool needs consistent attention. Our technicians understand the specific challenges Rio Verde pools face — from hard water mineral buildup to the rapid chlorine loss caused by UV exposure. We adjust our chemical treatments seasonally to keep your water balanced and inviting.

What's included: Surface skimming, wall & tile brushing, floor vacuuming, basket cleaning, chemical testing & balancing, equipment inspection, filter pressure check.

Green-to-Clean Pool Recovery

Come back from a long trip to find your pool has turned green? Don't panic. Our green-to-clean service will restore your pool to sparkling condition — usually within 48 to 72 hours. We've recovered hundreds of neglected pools across Rio Verde and the surrounding communities.

Our process includes shock treatment, algae elimination, thorough brushing, filter cleaning, and complete water rebalancing. We'll also diagnose what caused the issue to prevent it from happening again.

Pool Equipment Repair & Installation

From variable-speed pumps to energy-efficient heaters, we service and install all major brands of pool equipment. Our technicians carry common parts on their trucks so many repairs can be completed on the first visit.

Equipment we service: Pumps (single, dual, and variable speed), DE & cartridge filters, gas and heat pump heaters, chlorinators, automation systems, pool lights, valves, and plumbing.

Filter Cleaning & Replacement

A clean filter is essential for clear water and efficient circulation. We provide thorough filter cleans — whether you have a cartridge, DE, or sand filter. We recommend filter cleaning every 3-4 months in Rio Verde due to the high dust and debris common in the desert environment.

Salt System Installation & Service

Salt chlorine generators are increasingly popular in Rio Verde for their gentle, consistent chlorination. We install, maintain, and repair all major salt system brands including Pentair, Hayward, and Jandy. Our team will help you determine if a salt system is right for your pool and guide you through the transition.

Pool Automation & Smart Controls

Control your pool from your smartphone. We install and program automation systems that let you manage your pump, heater, lights, and water features from anywhere. Popular systems include Pentair IntelliCenter, Hayward OmniLogic, and Jandy iAqualink — all of which we're certified to install and service.
